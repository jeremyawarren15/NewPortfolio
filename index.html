About Me

How did I get here?

As a child I never truly appreciated that I was the go-to person to fix computer problems in my family. After a lot of retrospection I have come to realize that they came to me because I was almost always able to fix their problems. It is something that my wife and I discuss a lot and it is something that I see in my own sons; the ability to just know how something works.

Over the past five or so years I have been programming on and off, but have recently applied my skills in ways that I never have by getting trained at Eleven Fifty Academy. While I was at Indiana University I studied Informatics and Computer Science. That time at IU gave me some experience with Java, Python, and SQL.
