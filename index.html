About Me

I'm made to be a programmer. I know that a lot of people say that kind of thing, but I am convinced of it. Some of my earliest and most fond memories from my childhood are of computers. I remember getting my first computer. I was hooked from that point forward. Since that day I have always known that technology would be a part of what I do for the rest of my life.

I have had many opportunities to grow in my knowledge of technology. I first tried programming when I started high school. While I was at Indiana University I had the opportunity to learn Java and Python and to learn essential concepts of object oriented programming. I learned C# at Eleve Fifty Academy. Learning .NET with them changed my life and I can't wait to see where life takes me from here.
